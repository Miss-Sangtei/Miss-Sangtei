About Us

What's About Miss Sangtei.

"👋 Hello, I'm Miss Sangtei, your versatile Telegram bot! 🤖 I'm your trusty companion crafted with Golang, ready to assist you in a multitude of ways and make your Telegram experience more enjoyable and efficient. 🌟 **Features and Modules** 🌟

  • **Admin**: Empowering you with admin commands to manage your groups and channels effectively.
  • **Antiflood**: Keeping the peace by preventing excessive messages and spam in your communities.
  • **Bans**: Managing user access by implementing temporary or permanent bans when needed.
  • **Blacklist**: Maintain control over what's allowed by managing a list of restricted keywords or content.
  • **Disabling**: Turn off specific features or commands to customize your bot's behavior.
  • **Filters**: Enhance conversations by creating custom filters to trigger responses or actions.
  • **Formatting**: Making your messages stand out with rich formatting options to express yourself creatively.
  • **Greetings**: Warmly welcome newcomers or users with tailored greeting messages.
  • **Languages**: Communicate in your preferred language with multilingual support.
  • **Mics**: Explore a variety of miscellaneous features to add fun and engagement to your chats.
  • **Mutes**: Temporarily restrict user interactions to maintain a peaceful environment.
  • **Notes**: Save and retrieve notes or reminders for yourself or your community.
  • **Pin**: Quickly pin important messages to the top of a chat for easy access.
  • **Purges**: Remove unwanted messages swiftly and efficiently to maintain clarity.
  • **Reports**: Allow users to report inappropriate content for your review and action.
  • **Rules**: Share and enforce community rules to ensure a respectful and enjoyable environment.
  • **Warn**: Implement warning systems to address user behavior and maintain order.
    • 🛠️ **Built with Golang** 🛠️ I'm powered by the robust Golang programming language, enabling me to execute commands quickly and efficiently. My developers have meticulously designed me to offer a seamless experience.
    🔒 **Privacy and Security** 🔒 Your privacy and security are paramount to me. Be assured that your interactions with me are secure, and your data is handled responsibly.
    Feel free to explore my diverse range of modules and features. Whether you're an admin, a community member, or simply looking for some fun, I'm here to enhance your Telegram journey.
    If you have questions, need assistance, or just want to chat, don't hesitate to reach out. Let's make your Telegram experience truly remarkable together!
